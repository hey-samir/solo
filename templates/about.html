Frequently Asked Questions

To log a climb, tap the '+' button in the navigation bar. Fill in the details about your climb including the color, grade, and number of attempts. Mark it as 'Sent' if you completed the climb successfully.

A 'Send' means you successfully completed the climb from start to finish without falling. An 'Attempt' is when you tried the climb but didn't complete it. Both are valuable to track as they show your progress over time.

Points are calculated based on the difficulty of the climb and whether you sent it or not. Successfully sending a climb earns you 10 points multiplied by your rating, while attempts earn 5 points multiplied by your rating.

Currently, climbs cannot be edited or deleted after they're logged. We recommend double-checking your entries before submitting them to ensure accuracy.

Your statistics show your climbing progress including your success rate (percentage of successful sends), average grade (the typical difficulty level you climb), and points (overall achievement score based on your climbs).
